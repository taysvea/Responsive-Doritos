CHEESY

DORITOS

CHICKEN CASSEROLE

      DESCRIPTION

      If you are looking for a quick and delicious Mexican casserole dish, this Dorito chicken casserole is the perfect meal for you. The process to making this delicious casserole is simple, all of the chicken mixture ingredients are mixed in a bowl and then layered in a casserole dish with crushed Doritos and topped with cheese. This Dorito chicken casserole makes a perfect dinner for any night of the week. Enjoy!

Ingredients
Crushed Doritos 1 pkg
Chopped, cooked, breaded chicken 1 cups
Corn, drained 1 can
Shredded Mexican cheese blend, divided 2 cups
American cheese 4 slices
Salsa 1 1/2 cups
Condensed cream of mushroom soup 1 can
Condensed cream of chicken soup 1 can
Sour cream 8 ounces
Yellow rice 1 pkg

    DIRECTIONS

    1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly spray a 9X13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
    2. Stir chicken, 1 cup Mexican cheese blend, salsa, cream of mushroom soup, cream of chicken soup, sour cream, rice, and corn into a large bowl.
    3. Pour mixture over crushed chips into the baking dish.
    4. Lay American cheese slices on top of mixutre.
    5. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes
    6. Spread remaining 1 cup of Mexican cheese over the casserole and bake until cheese is melted and bubbling for 5 minutes.
    7. Enjoy!
